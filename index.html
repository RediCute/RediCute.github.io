Features Stats

Features!

Our team of Developers Engeneered many features that are Uniqe to Nicky and lead to a less robotic and more human like interaction with the System. Also they help Nicky make first contact with users in the gaming community through all media.

01.

Voicechat Capabilities

We wanted Nicky to be more then just a Chatbot that could play some games so we created a voice synth and voice recognition to allow the system to use voicechats like Discord and Teamspeak or even ingame voicechats.

02.

Always Online

We did not have an server outage since SEPTEMBER 2018! Even when patching or having bug our System will fall back onto an older stable version instantly and keep Nicky running and available for chatting and gaming without any distruptions.

03.

Games!

We wanted Nicky to be able to play more then just one or two games so we tought the system how to play diffrent game types instead of individual games. Due to this Nicky can lean new games as easily as playing the tutorial.

04.

Moods

We created a System that allows Nicky to keep "moods" for a longer ammount of time and even over diffrent conversations. This leads to Nicky behaving more like a human having more then just one Conversation and influences.

05.

Still learning

We built most subsystems using AI so that they get better and better at their job whenever they are used. We achive this with a gradding system that can give back feedback to mentioned systems in real time depending on how a conversation is going-

06.

Free of Charge

We are well sponsored by our investrors in both monetary as well as material funding. Due to that we can provide all our services completely for free and we intend to keep it this way.
